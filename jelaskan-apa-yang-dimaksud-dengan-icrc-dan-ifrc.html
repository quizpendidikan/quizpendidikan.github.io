1. apa perbedaan IFRC dan ICRC


International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) adalah federasi atau perkumpulan organisasi-organisasi palang merah nasional dari negara-negara di dunia.

Sementara, International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) adalah organisasi internasional berkantor di Jenewa, Swiss, yang mengkoordinasi kegiatan kemanusiaan di dunia, dalam gerakan Palang Merah.

Pembahasan:

Gerakan Palang Merah dimulai ketika Jean-Henri Dunant mendirikan gerakan kemanusiaan setelah menyaksikan korban-korban pertempuran Solferino pada tahun 1859 antara Austria dan Perancis.

Gerakan ini kemudian berkembang menjadi beberapa komponen organisasi yang saat ini melaksanakan kegiatan kemanusiaan.

Komite Internasional Palang Merah (International Committee of the Red Cross, ICRC) adalah lembaga kemanusiaan yang didirikan pada 1863 di Jenewa, Swiss, oleh Henry Dunant dan Gustave Moynier. Komite ini memiliki 25 anggota, yang memiliki kewenangan di bawah hukum internasional untuk melakukan kegiatan kemanusiaan, dan melindungi korban konflik bersenjata di dunia. ICRC dianugerahi Hadiah Nobel Perdamaian sebanyak tiga kali untuk jasa-jasanya dalam mengatasi dampak konflik (pada tahun 1917, 1944 dan 1963).  

Federasi Internasional Palang Merah dan Bulan Sabit Merah (International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies , IFRC) didirikan pada 1919 dan adalah federasi yang mengkoordinasikan kegiatan antara organisasi-organisasi Palang Merah dan Bulan Sabit Merah nasional di berbagai negara. Pada tingkat internasional, federasi memimpin dan mengatur serta bekerjasama dengan organisasi nasional, untuk melakukan misi bantuan bantuan menanggapi keadaan darurat berskala besar. Sekretariat federasi internasional ini berbasis di Jenewa, Swiss. Pada tahun 1963, federasi ini dianugerahi Hadiah Nobel Perdamaian bersama-sama dengan ICRC.

Analyze the language feature of the biography recount text!Jean-Henry Dunant or Henry dunant (1828-1910) was a swiss businessman and social activist, the founder of the red cross, and the first recipient of the nobel peace price. Jean-Henry Dunant, who was born in Geneva on 8 May 1828, to a prosperous businessman named Jean)Jaques Dunant and a social activist named Antoinette Dunant-Colladon. After incomplete secondary schooling, he was apprenticed to a Geneva bank. In 1853, he travelled to Algeria to take charge of the Swiss colony of Sétif. He started construction of a wheat mill, but could not obtain the land concession that was essential for its operation. After travelling to Tunisia he returned to Geneva, where he decided to approach Napoleon III to obtain the business document he needed. At the time, the Emperor was commanding the Franco-Sardinian tr oops fighting the Austrians in northern Italy, and it was there that Henry Dunant decided to seek him out. This was how he came to be present at the end of the battle of Solferino, in Lombardy. Returning to Geneva, he wrote A Memory of Solferino , which eventually led to the creation of the International Committee for Relief to the Wounded, the future International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC). Dunant was a member and acted as secretary. He was now famous and was received by heads of State, kings and princes of the European courts. But his financial affairs were floundering and he was declared bankrupt in 1867. Completely ruined, he was in debt for almost a million Swiss francs As a result of the scandal which this bankruptcy caused in Geneva, he resigned from his post as secretary of the International Committee. On 8 September 1867 the Committee decided to accept his resignation not only as secretary but also as a member. Dunant left for Paris, where he was reduced to sleeping on public benches. At the same time, however, the Empress Eugénie summoned him to the Tuileries Palace in order to consult him on extending the Geneva Convention to naval warfare. Dunant was made an honorary member of the national Red Cross societies of Austria, Holland, Sweden, Prussia and Spain. Tergetar oleh penderitaan tentara yang terluka, Henry Dunant bekerjasama dengan penduduk setempat, segera bertindak mengerahkan bantuan untuk menolong mereka.
