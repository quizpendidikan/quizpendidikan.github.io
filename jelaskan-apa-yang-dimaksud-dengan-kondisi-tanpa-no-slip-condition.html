3. Jelaskan apa yang dimaksud dengan condition of economy


Kredit yang diberikan juga perlu mempertimbangkan kondisi ekonomi yang dikaitkan dengan prospek usaha calon debitur. Ada suatu usaha yang sangat tergantung dari kondisi perekonomian, oleh karena itu perlu mengaitkan kondisi ekonomi dengan usaha calon debitur.
Permasalahan mengenai Condition of economy erat kaitannya dengan faktor politik, peraturan perundang-undangan negara dan perbankan pada saat itu serta keadaan lain yang mempengaruhi pemasaran seperti Gempa bumi, tsunami, longsor, banjir dsb.
Sebagai contoh beberapa saat yang lalu terjadi gejolak ekonomi yang bersifat negatif dan membuat nilai tukar rupiah menjadi sangat rendah, hal ini menyebabkan perbankan akan menolak setiap bentuk kredit invenstasi maupun konsumtif.

25. Jelaskan pengertian dan fungsi registration form, deposit slip, dan withdrawal slip menggunakan bahasa inggris


----Jawaban----
Registration form is a form which holds the information of the person who wants to register to join the event. It is usually used when joining an event, organization or clubs.

Deposit slip is a form which is given by the bank when we want to deposit funds to our bank account. It is used upon bank deposit.

Withdrawal slip is a form which is given by the bank when we want to withdraw funds from our bank account. It is used upon bank withdrawal.

Answer:
________
REGISTRATION FORM
The registration form is a form of form that we must fill out to inform others about who we are, and usually this registration form is filled in accordance with the identity card and other identification marks and serves to know where we are, our status, and others .

DEPOSIT SLIP
A deposit slip is a small piece of paper that we must fill in the bank when we want to deposit funds into a bank account. And its function so that we can deposit our funds to the bank after we fill some posts contained in the storage slip which includes: the date, name of the depositor, depositors account number, and the number of checks, cash, and coins are stored.

WITHDRAWEL SLIP
The withdrawal slip is a kind of small paper and the size is the same as the deposit slip contained in the bank. We must fill in the data in the withdrawal slip which includes the date of withdrawal of funds made, account number, and write down how much money will be withdrawn from the bank. Thus, the withdrawal slip basically serves as a required condition to withdraw funds from one's account.

27. Perbedaan purchase form,withdrawal slip,order form,deposit slip,reservation slip,Madical form dalam bahasa inggris


Ans:
// Purchase Form -- also called a purchase order or PO, is a form or set of form that is filled by a customer/client (buyer) that acts as a request to purchase goods, service, or product from a company/enterprise (seller). The company name, client's delivery address, object/service purchased, its agreed upon prices will be written on this form.

// Withdrawal Slip -- a piece of paper that acts as written order or an instruction given to a bank to withdraw funds from an existing account. The withdrawal amount, account number, owner's name and signature must be written while the withdrawal date will be written after the withdrawal process concludes.

// Order Form -- see purchase form above.

// Deposit Slip -- a piece of paper that is given when one deposits funds into an existing account. The deposit date, account owner's name, account number, and the amount deposited will be written on this slip, some deposit slip lists amounts acquired from banknotes, coins, and checks separately.

// Reservation Slip -- also called a booking form, is a form or set of form that is filled by a customer/client in order to reserve an object, the object range from entertainment tickets, transportation, accommodations, etc.

// Medical Form -- also called a medical history form or personal health record/PHR, is a form or set of form filled by a patient and given to a physician. The patient writes their recent illnesses, injuries, allergies, currently taken medications, their family health history, and past hospitalizations on this form. This form is used to give the patient the best medical care possible by avoiding allergy inducing medication, etc.
