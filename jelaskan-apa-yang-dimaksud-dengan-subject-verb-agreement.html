6. Buatlah 8 pernyaan tentang subject verb agreement?? Di Bantu yh teman2??


verb agreement in questions

In statements, the usual sentence structure is subject + verb. Questions, however, are arranged a little differently. In a question, at least part of the verb comes before the subject.

In the four examples below, the verbs are in bold. In each case, a helping verb comes before the subject, while the rest of the verb follows the subject. The subject is therefore between the two parts of the verb, and the structure is verb + subject + verb:

Has Karl been working on the project?  [verb has + subject Karl + verb been working]

Does Jan play basketball?  [verb does + subject Jan + verb play]

Have you seen the movie?  [verb have + subject you + verb seen]

Do they agree with your decision?  [verb do + subject they + verb agree]

The entire verb may even come before the subject. (This happens especially when the verb is a simple form of be: is, was, are, were.) Then the structure is verb + subject, as in the examples below:

Is Greta on your team?  [verb is + subject Greta]

Was the concert good?  [verb was + subject concert]

Are the children ready to go?  [verb are + subject children]

Were the keys in your pocket?  [verb were + subject keys]

This reversal of the usual subject-verb order is called inversion. Despite the fact that questions follow an inverted order, the subject and verb must still agree. In the examples above, the singular verbs has, does, is and was agree with their singular subjects Karl, Jan, Greta and concert. The plural verbs have, do, are and were agree with their plural subjects you, they, children and keys.

Remember, when you are asking a question, you need to make the verb agree with the subject that comes after it.

11. What is the meaning of subject verb agreement


Subject-verb agreement adalah persesuaian antara verb (kata kerja) dengan subject kalimat dalam hal number, yaitu: singular (tunggal) atau plural (jamak). Subjek dapat berupa noun (kata benda), pronoun (kata ganti), atau konstruksi lain yang berakting sebagai noun, seperti gerund dan infinitive. Pada dasarnya, singular subject (subjek tunggal) menggunakan singular verb (kata kerja tunggal), sedangkan plural subject (subjek jamak) menggunakan plural verb (kata kerja jamak).

ANSWER:

Concord, or subject-verb agreement, is an important aspect of grammar that ensures that the subject and verb in a sentence agree in terms of number and person. This can make or break the clarity and effectiveness of your writing, and as a writer, it is important to be mindful of this rule. In my recent holiday experience, Concord played a vital role in ensuring that my messages were well conveyed, whether I was writing emails to friends and family or posting on social media.

During my holiday, I visited several cities and towns across the country, exploring new sights and trying out new activities. From swimming in turquoise waters to hiking up mountains, my experiences were diverse and exciting. As someone who enjoys writing, I couldn't help but document my journey through various online platforms. I wrote posts on Facebook, shared photos on Instagram, and even sent out emails to my loved ones back home.

When writing these messages, I made sure to pay attention to Concord. I made sure that my subject and verbs were in agreement, ensuring that each sentence was clear and accurate. For example, in a post about my trip to the beach, I wrote "The waves crash against the shore as the sun sets in the distance." Here, the subject "waves" agrees with the verb "crash" in terms of number and person, making the sentence sound more fluid.

On another occasion, I explained how I went hiking with a group of people and encountered a beautiful waterfall. Instead of saying "The hikers see a waterfall," which is grammatically incorrect due to disagreement in subject and verb, I wrote "The hikers saw a waterfall." This way, the subject "hikers" agrees with the verb "saw" in terms of number and person, making the sentence more meaningful.

In addition, Concord also helped me convey my emotions more effectively. For example, in an email to my parents, I wrote, "I am having a great time exploring different parts of the country!" Here, the singular subject "I" agrees with the verb "am" in terms of number and person. This gave the message more credibility and emotional impact, conveying my genuine enthusiasm for my travel experience.

Overall, Concord played a vital role in my holiday experience, allowing me to convey my experiences and emotions more effectively. As a writer, I understand the importance of subject-verb agreement and always strive to ensure that my messages are grammatically correct, clear, and effective. This has helped me communicate my ideas more efficiently, whether through social media, email, or any other form of writing.
