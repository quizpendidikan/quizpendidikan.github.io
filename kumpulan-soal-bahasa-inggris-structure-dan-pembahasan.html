2. contoh biografi bahasa inggris yang ada structure parallels


Words and Phrases

With the -ing form (gerund) of words:

Parallel:

Mary likes hiking, swimming, and bicycling.

With infinitive phrases:

Parallel:

Mary likes to hike, to swim, and to ride a bicycle.
OR
Mary likes to hike, swim, and ride a bicycle.

(Note: You can use "to" before all the verbs in a sentence or only before the first one.)

Do not mix forms.

Example 1

Not Parallel: 
Mary likes hiking, swimming, and to ride a bicycle.

Parallel: 
Mary likes hiking, swimming, and riding a bicycle.

Example 2

Not Parallel: 
The production manager was asked to write his report quickly, accurately, and in a detailed manner.

Parallel: 
The production manager was asked to write his report quickly, accurately, and thoroughly.

Example 3

Not Parallel: 
The teacher said that he was a poor student because he waited until the last minute to study for the exam, completedhis lab problems in a careless manner, and his motivation was low.

Parallel: 
The teacher said that he was a poor student because he waited until the last minute to study for the exam, completedhis lab problems in a careless manner, and lacked motivation.

Clauses

A parallel structure that begins with clauses must keep on with clauses. Changing to another pattern or changing the voice of the verb (from active to passive or vice versa) will break the parallelism.

Example 1

Not Parallel: 
The coach told the players that they should get a lot of sleep, that they should not eat too much, and to do some warm-up exercises before the game.

Parallel: 
The coach told the players that they should get a lot of sleep, that they should not eat too much, and that they should do some warm-up exercises before the game.

— or —

Parallel: 
The coach told the players that they should get a lot of sleep, not eat too much, and dosome warm-up exercises before the game.

Example 2

Not Parallel: 
The salesman expected that he would presenthis product at the meeting, that there would be time for him to show his slide presentation, and that questions would be askedby prospective buyers. (passive)

Parallel: 
The salesman expected that he would presenthis product at the meeting, that there would be time for him to show his slide presentation, and that prospective buyers would ask him questions.

Lists After a Colon

Be sure to keep all the elements in a list in the same form.

Example 1

Not Parallel: 
The dictionary can be used to find these: word meanings, pronunciations, correct spellings, and looking up irregular verbs.

Parallel: 
The dictionary can be used to find these: word meanings, pronunciations, correct spellings, and irregular verbs.

5. Pengertian, tujuan, ciri2, dan generic structure dari descriptive teks dalam bahasa inggris besrta terjemahannya!


Pengertian Descriptive Text Descriptive text is the text to describe about thing, person, animals , place and so on. Jadi teks deskriptive text ditulis untuk mendeskripsikan benda, orang, hewan dan lain-lainnya yang dapat di deskripsikan. Deskipsi tentang orang biasanya menampilkan identitas orang tersebut serta ciri fisik, karakter, dab aktivitasnya. Deskripsi hewan biasanya tentang ciri-ciri fisik, tempat tinggal, kebiasaan dan kesukaannya. Sedangkan deskripsi tempat menerangkan letak/lokasi, bagian , ciri khusus dan hal yang menarik dari tempat tersebut. Tujuan Descriptive Text Descriptive text bertujuan untuk menggambarkan dan mengungkapkan atau mendeskripsikan ciri-ciri dari benda, orang, hewan , tempat dan lain-lainya. Desciptive text dibuat tanpa melakukan penelitian secara detail jadi penulis hanya menulis cerita descriptive text berdasarkan apa yang ia lihat dan ia dengar. Generic Structure dalam descriptive text Dalam menulis teks deskripsi kita harus memperhatikan beberapa hal berikut ini : Identification , yaitu bagian yang memperkenalkan tentang topik apa yang akan kita ceritakan dan memperkenalkan tokoh pemainnya Description , yaitu bagian yang menggambarkan atau mengungkapkan tentang ciri-ciri khusus , sifat, kesenangan , kebiasaan tokoh nya Ciri Kebahasaan dalam Descriptive text : Menggunakan simple present tense Menggunakan kata sifat atau adjective Menggunakan to be seperti (am,is,are) Menggambarkan object atau tokohnya dengan spesifik Menggunakan bahasa yang menarik

A long time ago, there were two animals, Sura and Baya. Sura was the name of a shark and Baya was a crocodile. They lived in a sea.
Once Sura and Baya were looking for some food. Suddenly, Baya saw a goat. “Yummy, this is my lunch,” said Baya.
“No way! This is my lunch. You are greedy” said Sura. Then they fought for the goat. After several hours, they were very tired.
Feeling tired of fighting, they lived in the different places. Sura lived in the water and Baya lived in the land. The border was the beach, so they would never fight again.
One day, Sura went to the land and looked for some food in the river. He was very hungry and there was not much food in the sea. Baya was very angry when he knew that Sura broke the promise.
They fought again. They both hit each other. Sura bit Baya’s tail. Baya did the same thing to Sura. He bit Sura very hard. Sura finally gave up and went back to the sea. Baya was happy.

Generic Structure :
1. Orientation: Pengenalan cerita biasanya bersisi siapa pelakau cerita, tempat terjadinya cerita dan kapan cerita itu terjadi.
"A long time ago, there were two animals, Sura and Baya."
2. Complication: Inilah hal utama yang harus ada pada semua cerita yang masuk kategori teks narrative bahasa Inggris. Complication adalah pertentangan antar para pelaku cerita.
"Then they fought for the goat.
Baya was very angry when he knew that Sura broke the promise."
3. Resolution: Semua cerita harus diakahiri pertentangannya. Dari sudut pelaku utama, resolution ini bisa berbentu happy ending pun juga sad ending.
"Sura finally gave up and went back to the sea. Baya was happy."

10. Apa yang di bahas dalam structure and written expression


Jawaban:

Structure and written expression adalah topik bahasa Inggris yang membahas tentang struktur kalimat dan tata bahasa serta gaya penulisan. Topik ini penting untuk dipelajari oleh para pelajar bahasa Inggris, baik pemula maupun lanjutan.

Dalam structure and written expression, kita akan mempelajari tentang:

> Struktur kalimat, termasuk jenis-jenis kalimat, subjek dan predikat, kata kerja, kata benda, kata sifat, kata keterangan, dan konjungsi.

> Tata bahasa, termasuk aturan-aturan penggunaan kata, frasa, dan klausa.

> Gaya penulisan, termasuk pemilihan kata, struktur kalimat, dan penggunaan tanda baca.

Pemahaman yang baik tentang structure and written expression akan membantu kita untuk:

> Menghasilkan kalimat yang efektif dan jelas.

> Menghindari kesalahan tata bahasa.

> Menulis dengan gaya yang menarik dan mudah dibaca.

Berikut adalah beberapa contoh topik yang bisa dibahas dalam structure and written expression:

> Jenis-jenis kalimat, seperti kalimat simple, compound, complex, dan compound-complex.

> Subjek dan predikat, dan bagaimana menentukan subjek dan predikat dalam sebuah kalimat.

> Kata kerja, dan berbagai jenis kata kerja beserta fungsinya.

> Kata benda, dan berbagai jenis kata benda beserta fungsinya.

> Kata sifat, dan berbagai jenis kata sifat beserta fungsinya.

> Kata keterangan, dan berbagai jenis kata keterangan beserta fungsinya.

> Konjungsi, dan berbagai jenis konjungsi beserta fungsinya.

> Gaya penulisan, seperti pemilihan kata, struktur kalimat, dan penggunaan tanda baca.

11. carilah contoh teks fabel dengan bahasa inggris beserta generic structure dan mora lvalue


The Ant And The Grasshopper
In a field one summer’s day a grasshopper was hopping about, chirping and singing to its heart’s content. An ant passed by, bearing along with great toil an ear of corn he was taking to the nest.
“Why not come and chat with me,” said the grasshopper, “instead of toiling and moiling in that way?”
“I am helping to lay up food for the winter,” said the ant, “and recommend you to do the same.”
“Why bother about winter?” said the grasshopper; we have got plenty of food at present.” The Ant went on its way and continued its toil.
When the winter came the grasshopper found itself dying of hunger while it saw the ants distributing, every day, corn and grain from the stores they had collected in the summer.
Generic structure Contoh Narrative Text Fable
1. Orientation: First information of the participants inside the story. From the story about ant and grasshopper below, we see the orientation as:
In a field one summer’s day a Grasshopper was hopping about. An Ant passed by…
2. Complication: What happens next among the participants of the story. This phase introduce a problem existing between or among the participants. From the example of narrative below, we see the complication is:
“Why bother about winter?” said the Grasshopper; we have got plenty of food at present.” But the Ant went on its way and continued its toil.
3. Resolution: The way a writer ends his story plot. It can be successful ending or failure one which leads to sad ending story. From the story about ant and grasshopper, we see the writer give the ending as follow:
When the winter came the Grasshopper found itself dying of hunger.

14. contoh contoh explanation tentang virus Corona beserta generic structure dalam bahasa Inggris beserta bahasa Indonesia ​


Jawaban:

Identification:

December 2019, the world was shocked with an outbreak of a respiratory virus in the city of Wuhan, the capital city of Hubei province of the republic of China, recently known as the novel coronavirus or COVID-19. This virus belongs to a group of coronavirus that can infect human. The virus spread so rapidly that it has become a worldwide pandemic within only two months.

Description:

The name “Coronavirus” is given to this group of viruses based on their appearance under a microscope. The word “Corona (the Latin of “Crown”)” is chosen because their appearance is similar to a solar corona with spikes around their outer membrane which makes it look like an actual crown especially with their spherical shape. Their size is around 120 nm (nanometer). There isn’t any way for us yet to tell the actual color of a coronavirus since no official site released the information up to this moment. But, you may find some pictures on the internet where it is colored red or yellow.

This virus affects differently toward different people. Some may develop mild to moderate symptoms which may not required special treatment from the paramedic and some may not even be affected at all although they are already exposed to the virus. Usually, this happens to people with strong immunity system. But, the effect can also be severe which might ends with death. There are several signs that we can use to tell when someone is infected by COVID-19. These symptoms include fever, cough, shortness of breath, tiredness, sore throat, even diarrhea and runny nose.

This virus spread so easily through droplets from coughs and sneezes. It might expose anyone within 1.5 meters radius from the person who is already infected by this virus and turn them into virus carrier. The worst part of all this is that we may not realize that we have been infected until it is too late, because most cases show that symptoms are showing up within 14 days of exposure to the virus and within these days we may have infected more people. That is the reason why, governments in some countries decided to undergo lockdown or social distancing method in the spirit of stopping the virus spread.

21. lagu bahasa inggris beserta arti dan generic structurenya​


Untuk menjawab pertanyaan tersebut, kita akan menggunakan lagu "All of Me" yang dinyanyikan oleh John Legend.

Lagu "All of Me" memiliki struktur lagu (generic structure) sebagai berikut:

  Intro - Verse - Pre Chorus - Chorus - Verse - Pre Chorus - Chorus - Bridge - Chorus - Outro

Lagu ini adalah sebuah lagu romantis yang liriknya mengandung makna ungkapan hati John Legend untuk Chrissy Teigen, istrinya. Liriknya menceritakan tentang cinta seseorang yang menerima ketidaksempurnaan pasangannya.

Pembahasan

Struktur lagu atau generic structure of a song adalah sebuah susunan bagian-bagian musik yang menghasilkan kesatuan komposisi musik yang utuh dan bermakna. Sebuah lagu memiliki beberapa bagian, seperti:

Intro, yaitu bagian awal atau pengantar sebuah lagu.Verse atau bait, yaitu bagian awal nyanyian yang berisi lirik. Biasanya, satu lagu memiliki dua sampai tiga bait.Chorus atau refrein, yaitu bagian puncak nyanyian yang berisi pesan utama atau inti kisah yang disajikan dalam lirik lagu.Bridge, yaitu bagian yang digunakan untuk menjembatani bagian-bagian lagu. Pada umumnya, bagian bridge dipakai untuk mengantar lagu kepada perubahan nada dasar, tempo, dan sebagainya.Coda atau outro, yaitu bagian yang mengakhiri atau menutup lagu.

Kadang, sebuah lagu juga memiliki pre-chorus atau post-chorus, yaitu sebuah bait pendek yang berfungsi untuk menjembatani chorus, baik sebelum chorus dimulai (pre chorus) atau setelah chorus selesai (post chorus).

24. 2. Tuliskan generic structure dan pengertian masing2 dalam bahasa Inggris dan bahasa indonesia​


Generic structure dari narrative text:

Orientation (pendahuluan) - pada bagian ini biasanya diceritakan awal mula dan perkenalan tokoh-tokohnya beserta tempat dan waktu kejadian.

Complication - bagian ini menceritakan tentang ketika permasalahan antar tokoh karakter muncul.

Resolution - bagaimana penyelesaian masalah pada bagian complication diatasi di bagian ini.

Re-orientation - adalah rangkuman dan penutup dari cerita narasi.

Pembahasan

Narrative text adalah jenis bacaan yang berupa cerita khayalan, kisah nyata yang direkayasa, atau dongeng.

Tujuan: Untuk menghibur para pembaca.

Karakteristik:

Menggunakan pola kalimat lampau (simple past tense)

Diawali dengan kata keterangan waktu (adverbial of time) contohnya seperti long time ago, once upon a time, dll.

Menggunakan kata sambung (conjunction) seperti and then, before, after, dll.

28. deskripsi kelas bahasa inggris menggunakan generic structure​


Descriptive Text

Descriptive text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu (manusia, hewan, tumbuhan dan benda) secara khusus atau spesifik.

Generic Structure dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Identification: Berisi tentang penjelasan singkat dari apa yang akan di deskripsikan.

2. Description: Mendeskripsikan benda, hean, tumbuhan dan manusia secara spesifik atau khusus.

Language features dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Menggunakan simple present tense

2. Menggunakan kata yang spesifik atau tidak terlalu umum

3. Menggunakan adjective untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu

Pembahasan

English Class

I have a fun English class. Our teacher, Miss Selvi, always teach us by using game. She delivers the material interestingly. We are often given some flash card to memorize vocabularies. We usually play 'catch the ball' game. The class is divided into group of four or five students. Each group will be given a question, and then group members must pick up the ball in the middle of table quickly. Group member who is the quickest to pick the ball, will answer the question and get point. The member who has the highest poin is the winner of each group. It is very nice game that makes our English class interesting.
