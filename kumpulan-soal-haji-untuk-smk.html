21. Ada yang bisa bantu soal SMK


a = 102
r = 34/102 = 1/3
Un = 34/6.561

[tex]Un \: = a. {r}^{n - 1} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 102. \: {( \frac{1}{3}) }^{n - 1} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 102. {( \frac{1}{3} })^{n} . ({ \frac{1}{3} })^{ - 1} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 102 \times 3 \times {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} = 306 \times {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{34}{6.561} \times \frac{1}{306} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{ \cancel{34} }{6.561} \times \frac{1}{ \cancel{306} \: {}^{9} } = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \frac{1}{6.561} \times \frac{1}{9} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ {3}^{ - 8} \times {3}^{ - 2} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ {3}^{ - 10} = {3}^{ - n} \\ \\ \\ \\ - n = - 10 \\ \\ n = 10[/tex]

maka,banyak suku barisan tersebut adalah 10
