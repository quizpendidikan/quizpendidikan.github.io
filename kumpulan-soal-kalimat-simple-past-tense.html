Simple Past Tense

Apa itu Simple Past Tense?

Simple Past Tense adalah kalimat sederhana yang menunjukkan kegiatan/tindakan yang terjadi di masa lalu.

Rumus Simple Past Tense :

Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Subject + Did/Was/Were + Not + Verb 1 + Object

Did + Subject + Infitinitive Verb + Object + ?

Was/Were + Subject + Adjective/Adverb + ?

Contoh :

- Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Example : I met my cousin last week.

She went to the hospital two days ago.

- Subject + Did/Was/Were + Not + Verb 1 + Object

Example : I didn't go to school three days ago.

She wasn't play with her friends last night.

- Did + Subject + Infinitive Verb + Object

Example : Did you go to the market alone?

Did she find something to eat?

- Was/Were + Subject + Adjective + Adverb

Example : Was Alya sad because of him?

Were they bored at home?

16. Apakah simple past tense dengan past tense sama? Dan berikan rumus serta contoh kalimatnya


Penjelasan:

atas contoh

bawah rumus

Ya.

Rumus Active voice :

Positive form : Subject + The second form of the verb + Object + Time Signal

Contoh : John kicked the ball last game

Negative form : Subject + didn't + verb + object + Time Signal

Contoh : Bob didn't eat dinner last Wednesday

Interrogative form : Did + Subject + verb + object + Time Signal + ?

Contoh : Did you report the problem yesterday?

Rumus Passive voice :

Positive form : Subject + was / were + the third form of the verb / adverb + Time signal

Contoh : You were graduated last year

Negative form : Subject + wasn't / weren't + the third form of the verb / adverb + time signal

Contoh : Mary wasn't happy just now

Interrogative form : Was / were + Subject + the third form of the verb / adverb + time signal + ?

Contoh : Was it the book yesterday?

19. Buat lah 5 kalimat simple past tense, simple present tense, dan simple present continuous! (+,-,?) ​


Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense

1) The gardener trimmed the flowers last week.

2) The police officer explained the situation this morning.

3). My mother painted her old vases three days ago.

4) The sailors sang a happy song last month.

5) My dog ran around my house yesterday morning.

Simple Present Tense

1) The gardener usually trims the flowers when they grow too high.

2).The police officer regularly explains the situation to the press.

3) My mother always paints her old vases to make it look new again

4) The sailors sing a happy song whenever they are happy.

5) My dog runs around my house everyday.

Simple Present Continuous Tense

1) The gardener is triming the flowers at the moment

2) The police officer is explaining the situation to the press.

3). My mother is painting one of her old vases.

4) The sailors are singing a happy song.

5) My dog is running around my house now.

Penjelasan:

Simple Past Tense adalah tense dalam bahasa Inggris yang dipergunakan ketika menceritakan sesuatu yang dikerjakan atau terjadi di masa lampau (masa sebelum saat ini).

Misalnya kita hendak mengatakan Saya membeli sebuah novel, tapi saya membelinya Sabtu kemarin (masa lampau), maka dalam bahasa Inggris tense yang dipakai adalah simple past tense.

Kalimatnya adalah: I bought a novel last Saturday.

Dengan  simple past tense ini, walaupun kalimatnya tidak menyebut soal waktu secara spesifik, tapi dengan penggunaan tense ini pendengar atau pembaca akan segera memahami bahwa yang sedang dibicarakan adalah sesuatu yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Misalnya: I bought a novel

Walaupun tidak dijelaskan kapan si penulis membeli novel itu, pembaca memahami bahwa dia membelinya di masa lampau.

Simple Present Tense adalah tense yang dipakai untuk menceritakan atau membicarakan suatu peristiwa atau keadaan yang:

- bersifat umum atau kebenaran umum

- terjadi berulang-ulang / kebiasaan

- bersifat ajakan atau perintah

Simple Present Continuous Tense dipergunakan untuk menceritakan suatu kejadian yang pada saat ini (pada saat si pembicara mengucapkan kalimat ini) masih tengah berlangsung (belum selesai / belum usai).

PEMBAHASAN MATERI

ʕ Simple Past Tense ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang terjadi atau dilakukan di masa yang telah berlalu.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Complement(–) Subject + didn't + Verb 1 + Object/Complement(?) Did + Subject + Verb 1 + Object/Complement + ?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Continuous ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang sedang terjadi atau berlangsung di masa yang telah berlalu. Misalnya: Saya sedang menulis berita saat kamu datang kemarin.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + was/were + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(–) Subject + was/were + not + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(?) Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing + Object/Complement + ?

ᴥ Notes:

Was, digunakan pada subject: I, he, she, dan it. Were, digunakan pada subject: You, we, dan they.

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Future ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang akan terjadi namun dengan persepsi masa lalu.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + would + Verb 1 + Object/Complement(–) Subject + wouldn't + Verb 1 + Object/Complement(?) Would + Subject + Verb 1 + Object/Complement + ?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Perfect ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang telah terjadi di masa lalu di mana waktu terjadinya dijelaskan secara spesifik. Misalnya: Aku telah menyelesaikan tugas sekolah ku sebelum Ibu menelpon ku.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + had + Verb 3 + Object/Complement(–) Subject + hadn't + Verb 3 + Object/Complement(?) Had + Subject + Verb 3 + Object/Complement + ?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Perfect Continuous ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang telah terjadi di masa lalu di mana kehiatan tersebut masih berlangsung di masa tersebut.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + had + been + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(–) Subject + hadn't + been + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(?) Had + Subject + been + Verb-ing + Object/Complement + ?
