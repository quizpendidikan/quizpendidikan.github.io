20. ada yang bisa btw ini bukan pat soal latihan ​


Jawaban:

Turunan

s = 2t³ - 6t + 3

s′(t) = 6t² - 6

syarat s′(t) = 0

6t² - 6 = 0

6t² = 6

t = √6/6

t = √1 = 1

subsitusi

s(t) = 2t³ - 6t + 3

s(1) = 2(1)³ - 6(1) + 3

s(1) = 2 - 6 + 3

s(1) = -1 m/det'
